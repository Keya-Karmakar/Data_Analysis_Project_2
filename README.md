# 🌱 ESG Sustainability Intelligence System & Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **ESG Sustainability Intelligence System** is an advanced Data Analytics project designed to evaluate corporate sustainability performance using Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) indicators.

The project combines **NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, KPI Engineering, Risk Analytics, and Business Intelligence** to transform raw sustainability data into actionable insights.

A comprehensive **ESG Sustainability Intelligence Dashboard** was also developed to provide executives, investors, analysts, and sustainability teams with a visual overview of organizational sustainability performance and future risk exposure.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Measure sustainability performance using ESG indicators.
* Build intelligent sustainability KPIs.
* Analyze carbon efficiency and environmental impact.
* Evaluate workforce wellbeing and human capital strength.
* Identify high-risk organizations through risk analytics.
* Benchmark industries based on ESG performance.

# 📊 Key KPIs Developed

## 1. Carbon Efficiency

Measures how efficiently revenue is generated relative to carbon emissions.

Formula:

Revenue ÷ Carbon Emission

Higher values indicate better environmental efficiency.

---

## 2. Employee Wellbeing Index

Combines:

* Employee Satisfaction
* Training Hours

Used to assess workforce development and employee engagement.

---

## 3. ESG Score

A composite sustainability score calculated using:

* Renewable Energy Adoption
* Employee Diversity
* Governance Performance
* Employee Wellbeing
* Workplace Safety

Higher ESG scores indicate stronger sustainability performance.

---

## 4. Risk Score

Measures sustainability-related business risks using:

* Carbon Emissions
* Safety Incidents
* Governance Weaknesses

Higher scores indicate greater organizational risk.

---

## 5. ESG Innovation Index

Measures sustainability-driven innovation and future readiness.

---

## 6. Sustainability Momentum

Evaluates long-term sustainability growth potential.

---

## 7. Human Capital Strength

Assesses organizational workforce quality and development.

---

## 8. Resilience Score

Measures the ability to withstand future sustainability and operational challenges.
